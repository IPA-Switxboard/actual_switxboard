ABOUT Co-Creation Labs

Local Participants
Description

In our mission to rethink international development through connectivity and accountability IPA created the «Co-Creation Labs». Connected through the IPA network, our co-creation labs bring together local ideas, projects or interested people and national and international experts on a specific topic. Under the coordination of a experienced and skilled facilitator the participants will work towards a specific outcome that solves a problem or improves existing opportunities.
Local people with:

  • Existing projects
  • Ideas that wait to be implemented
  • personal interest but lack the means or the know-how to advance.
