An innovative approach toward irregular migration

Tunisia being a country of destination and transit for migration from neighbourhood and sub-Saharan African countries, the rise of irregular migration raises new questions about ways to address effectively this phenomenon.

The “Harraga” project aims to better support those who are in particular need of protection, but also to develop solutions and alternative perspectives for target groups to support sustainable development objectives #Learn #Connect #Adapt

The measures and activities of this project will address root causes of irregular migration and develop new perspectives through better understanding and better contact with the following people of concern:

  • - Vulnerable migrants residing in Tunisia
  • - Tunisian voluntarily and involuntarily returnees
  • - Potential irregular migration candidates
