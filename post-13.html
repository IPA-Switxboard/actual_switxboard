We honoured today with the visit of Dr. Sibylle Stier the Music Therapy professor at the Musikhochschule of Augsburg Germany and Manuel Linke a young film maker from the Music Can Help organization in Germany to develop with a group of young Sub Saharian migrants and Tunisians a common theatre project.
The partnership agreement aims to promote to create a Music Therapy theatrical performance with the participation of the young Tunisian artists and Sub Saharian migrants in Tunisia.
This project is co-financed by the Asylum and Migration Fund of the European Union and the Federal Ministry of Austria.
